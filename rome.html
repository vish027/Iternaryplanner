Rome

Rome, the Eternal City, is one of the world's most historic and culturally significant destinations. As the capital of Italy, it boasts landmarks like the Colosseum, the Pantheon, and St. Peter's Basilica. Known for its ancient ruins, stunning architecture, and vibrant culture, Rome is a living museum that showcases the legacy of the Roman Empire and Renaissance artistry. Visitors can wander through cobblestone streets, savor authentic Italian cuisine, and explore iconic sites such as the Vatican City, Trevi Fountain, and Piazza Navona. A trip to Rome is an unforgettable journey into history, art, and timeless beauty.
