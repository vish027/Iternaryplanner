Grand Canyon

The Grand Canyon is one of the most iconic natural wonders of the world. Located in Arizona, USA, it stretches for over 277 miles (446 kilometers) and is up to 18 miles (29 kilometers) wide. Known for its breathtaking vistas, steep red rock walls, and the Colorado River flowing through it, the Grand Canyon has been carved by millions of years of geological activity. Visitors can explore its beauty through hiking trails, helicopter tours, rafting adventures, and panoramic viewpoints like the South Rim and the North Rim. With over six million visitors annually, the Grand Canyon is a testament to the raw power of nature and its ability to create awe-inspiring landscapes.
