Top Attractions in the USA

Statue of Liberty

The Statue of Liberty is one of the most recognizable symbols of freedom and democracy in the world. Located on Liberty Island in New York Harbor, it was gifted to the United States by France in 1886. Standing at 151 feet tall (93 meters), the statue holds a torchabove the city, welcoming immigrants arriving by sea. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and attracts millions of visitors every year.

Grand Canyon

The Grand Canyon is one of the most famous natural wonders in the world, located in the state of Arizona. This vast and breathtaking canyon is carved by the Colorado River and stretches for over 277 miles (446 kilometers). It reaches depths of over a mile and showcases a stunning array of rock formations. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and attracts millions of visitors each year for hiking, sightseeing, and rafting.
